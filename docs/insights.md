# Key Insights

* Higher ad frequency (>0.5 ads/min) significantly increases user drop-off, indicating ad overload negatively impacts engagement.
* Repeating the same ad more than 3 times leads to a sharp decline in next-day retention (creative fatigue effect).
* Ads shown immediately after pause disrupt user control moments and increase session abandonment.
* Increasing ad volume reduces content completion rates, breaking the viewing experience flow.

# Recommendations

* Implement dynamic ad frequency caps based on session duration
* Introduce ad creative rotation to prevent repetition fatigue
* Avoid serving ads immediately after pause/resume actions
* Optimize ad placement around natural content breakpoints
